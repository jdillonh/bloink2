help

bloink2 is a game about making music by causing things to run into each other.

Click and Drag to make a new object or move an existing one, Use the Scroll Wheel to rotate objects.

Press x to clear projectiles.
Press c to clear everything.

The paddle is just here to get hit.
It makes no sound when hit.

The Circle Emitter shoots circle projectiles.
It makes a low, round sound when hit.

The Square Emitter shoots square projectiles.
It makes a low, buzzy sound when hit.

The Circle Projectile comes from the circle emitter.
It is effected by gravity, and makes a high, round sound on impact.

The Square Projectile comes from the square emitter.
It is effected by gravity, and makes a high, buzzy sound on impact.
